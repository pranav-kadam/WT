Courses Offered

The courses are offered in the six streams of Engineering

Undergraduate Programmes

WCE offers following branches B.Tech Programmes at the Undergraduate Level:

1. B.Tech In Electronics Engineering
2. B.Tech In Computer Science And Engineering
3. B.Tech In Electrical Engineering
4. B.Tech In Mechanical Engineering
5. B.Tech In Information Technology
6. B.Tech In Civil Engineering

Postgraduate Programmes

At the Post-Graduate(M.Tech.) level WCE has the following programs:

1. M.Tech. In Computer Science
2. M.Tech. In Signal Processing
3. M.Tech. In Embedded System & VLSI
4. M.Tech. In Industrial Electronics

Our Facilities

A wide of range of facilities have been provided to the students to ensure a conducive learning atmosphere as well as a pleasant stay in campus.
WCE ensures that various aspects of student welfare have been well taken care of.

Central Library

Ajut Gulabchand Central Library of Walchand College of Engineering is functioning from 1st & Mezzanine Floors of Library Building. Library Building is centrally situated.

Sports

WCE has a range of facilities including a football ground, cricket ground, basketball court, badminton court, volleyball court, and indoor gymnasium. There is also a running/jogging track on the campus.

Food & Canteens

In addition to the regular mess facilities in the hostels, the WCE campus boasts of affordable canteens spread throughout the campus. All canteens provide delicious food at reasonable prices.
